Cirque du Soleil

Case Study
Annual Bilingual Report
Theme: Illumination
Technique: pencil crayon, acrylics, desktop publishing

This is a fourteen page annual bilingual (French and English) publication that Cirque Du Soleil distributes. The school challenge was to come up with illustrations that would demonstrate the Illumination theme. The stained glass acrobats perfectly reflect this objective. It also gives the publication an unusual twist that captivates the reader and encourages readership of a report that is filled with lots of data.
